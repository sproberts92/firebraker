Fire resistant

FIREBRAKER is the first and most ground-breaking product created by the Think'nSmart entity. FIREBRAKER's main applications is in bush fire fighting and prevention, and can be much more efficient at fire prevention than water*.

Organic

Unlike most other fire retarding products, FIREBRAKER is completely food grade and organic. There is no limit as to the treatment possibilities for you to protect your property, possessions and loved ones from fire.

Non-toxic

FIREBRAKER is the leading non-toxic treatment technology for fire proofing and retarding, applicable on any porous material including timber, insulation, fencing and even grass or foliage.
